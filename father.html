Churamani Pandeya

Academic Qualification

Churamani Pandeya is a well-educated professional with a strong academic foundation in education. He holds a Master's degree in Education Leadership & Management from the School of Education, KU, and a Master's degree in English Language Teaching from Kathmandu Shiksha Campus, TU. His Bachelor's degree in English Language Teaching from Butwal Multiple Campus, TU, and Diploma in Education from the same institution further solidify his expertise in the field. This impressive academic profile, coupled with his extensive professional experience, positions Churamani Pandeya as a highly qualified and knowledgeable educator.

Professional Experience

With a distinguished career spanning over two decades, Churamani Pandeya has made significant contributions to the field of education. He has held diverse leadership roles, including Senior School In-charge, Middle School In-charge, Head of Department, and Senior Teacher at renowned institutions. His expertise in English Language Teaching is evident through his roles as a Senior Lecturer, Senior English Teacher, and Faculty Head. He has also actively engaged in teacher training programs and served as a consultant for community education initiatives.

Key Strengths and Achievements:

  • Leadership: Proven ability to lead and manage educational institutions.
  • Pedagogical Expertise: Strong foundation in English Language Teaching and effective instructional strategies.
  • Professional Development: Active engagement in teacher training and continuous learning.
  • Community Engagement: Dedication to community education and outreach programs.
  • Extracurricular Involvement: Passion for arts, culture, and student development.
