Ritesh Panthee

Professional Experience

Ritesh Panthee has a solid academic foundation in law. He is currently pursuing a B.A.LL.B degree at National Law College, Nepal, and is in his eighth semester. Before this, he completed his +2 in Law at Kalika Manabgyan Higher Secondary School in Butwal, Rupandehi, achieving a cumulative GPA of 3.01. His foundational schooling concluded with the School Leaving Certificate (SEE) from Navin Aaudhyogic Kadar Bahadur Rita Higher Secondary School in Butwal, Rupandehi, with a GPA of 2.7. This academic background reflects his dedication to understanding and practicing law.
